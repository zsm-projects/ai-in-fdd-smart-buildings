The Right Tool for the Job: A Spectrum of AI

Expert Systems (ES)

The foundation of trust. Uses physics-based logic and engineering rules to accurately diagnose complex problems. This is the core of reliable FDD.

Machine Learning (ML)

The efficiency engine. Uses statistical algorithms to learn patterns, automating repetitive tasks like cleaning up and classifying inconsistent data points.

Large Language Models (LLM)

The natural interface. Enables intuitive interaction with data through text prompts, summarizing insights and orchestrating complex workflows.
